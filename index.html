Lana Del Rey

About

Lana Del Rey is an American indie rock singer-songwriter.

She was born on June 21st, 1985 and is 37 years old.

She has been active as a singer since 2005 and has released 8 studio albums so far with her 9th album set to release this year on March 24th.
